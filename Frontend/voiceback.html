Platform Features

📝

Voices From the Ground

Submit stories, issues, or observations related to gender inequality or social injustice. Add images, text, or videos to illustrate your point. Stay anonymous if needed for your safety.

📡

Amplification Engine

Submissions are tagged with SDGs and sent to relevant NGOs in the GNEC network. Option to share with UN bodies or media for greater impact. Community upvoting shows urgency.

🔁

Feedback Loop

NGOs or authorities respond with actions they've taken. Contributors can track if their voice led to change. Creates transparency and builds trust in the system.

🧠

Smart Clustering

AI-powered dashboard groups reports by region, type, and urgency. Helps GNEC identify trends and deploy support efficiently to where it's needed most.

How It Works

1

Submit Your Voice

Share your story of inequality or injustice using our simple form. Add media and choose whether to remain anonymous.

2

Get Amplified

Your submission is tagged and shared with relevant NGOs who can help. Others can upvote to show urgency.

3

Receive Feedback

NGOs respond with actions they've taken. Track progress and see the impact of your voice.

4

Create Change

Your story joins others to reveal patterns and drive systemic change through GNEC's network.
